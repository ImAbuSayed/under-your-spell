[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the static every time you look my way
A heavy gravity that pulls me through the gray
The shadows melt when you are standing in the frame
I lose my balance every time you call my name

[Verse 1]
We let the evening settle soft against the floor
A glowing ember that we leave outside the door
The amber lighting paints a story in your eyes
A slow surrender hiding underneath the skies

[Pre-Chorus]
The air is heavy with the things we never say
But every motion gives the secret right away
A magnetic tension that I never want to break
I am unraveling with every breath you take

[Chorus]
I am entirely under your spell
Falling deeper than I could ever tell
Caught in the gravity you make me feel
A sweet surrender that is finally real

[Post-Chorus]
Under your spell, losing my mind
The sweetest illusion that I could ever find

[Verse 2]
A trace of perfume like a memory in the dark
A sudden wildfire coming from a single spark
We leave the noisy crowded places far behind
To find the quiet where our paths are intertwined

[Bridge]
Under your spell, I am letting go
A quiet magic that starts to grow
I see the future waking up inside your gaze
We leave the past to burn away within the haze
Even Saeed is trading armor for the fall
I lean into the fire and surrender to it all

[Spoken]
(There is a quiet storm whenever you are near)
(I am letting go, and letting you take over here)
